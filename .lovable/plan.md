

# Sweet Cleaner – Landing Page

## Overview
A stunning single-page landing site for Sweet Cleaner, an eco-commercial cleaning company in Gauteng, featuring their uploaded logo prominently.

## Design System
- **Primary green**: #10B981, **Dark accent**: #047857, **Background**: white/cream tones
- Glassmorphism cards, smooth scroll animations, eco-friendly aesthetic
- Logo displayed large in navbar and hero section

## Sections

### 1. Sticky Navigation
- Large Sweet Cleaner logo (uploaded image) + nav links (Services, Why Us, Packages, Contact)
- Smooth scroll, mobile hamburger menu

### 2. Hero
- Bold headline: "Sweet Cleaner: Office Perfection Starts Here"
- Tagline from logo: "Taste the sweetness of immaculate clean"
- 3 service icons (Office, Window, Ceiling/Wall)
- Green WhatsApp CTA button for free quote
- Large logo displayed prominently

### 3. Services (3 Interactive Cards)
- **Office Cleaning**: Desks, floors, restrooms – daily/weekly contracts
- **Window Cleaning**: Interior/exterior, high-access streak-free shine
- **Ceiling & Wall Cleaning**: Dust/vents removal, high-reach pro tools
- Glassmorphism hover effects with scale animations

### 4. Why Choose Us
- 4 feature cards: Eco Plant-Based Products, Trained Commercial Team, Flexible Scheduling, Before/After Results
- Icons and subtle fade-in animations

### 5. Testimonials
- 4-5 rotating B2B testimonial cards with star ratings
- Quotes from office managers, retail owners, factory supervisors

### 6. Packages
- 3 pricing tiers: Basic (R500), Full Service (R1,200), Premium (R2,000+)
- Highlighted "Most Popular" middle card
- WhatsApp CTA on each package

### 7. Contact Section
- Contact form (name, email, service dropdown, message)
- Embedded Google Maps placeholder for Johannesburg
- Business details and WhatsApp link

### 8. Floating WhatsApp Button
- Fixed green WhatsApp icon bottom-right
- Toggle open/close with pre-filled message

## SEO
- Proper title, meta description, and keyword meta tags in index.html

## Technical
- Fully responsive, mobile-first
- Smooth scroll animations using CSS/Intersection Observer
- Copy uploaded logo to src/assets for use throughout
